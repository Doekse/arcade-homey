Ever thought to yourself "Man, I'd wish I could play my favorite msDOS games in Homey Dashboards" No? Me neither, but now you can! Yes, it does run DOOM... and more ...

HomeyArcade turns your dashboard into a tiny retro machine with a growing library of classic shareware-era games, playable right inside a widget. Pick a game, pick a console skin, and start playing without leaving Homey.

All bundled titles are historical shareware/freeware distributions from their original rights holders.
